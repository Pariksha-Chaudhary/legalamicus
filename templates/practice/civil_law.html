Civil Law Practice Areas

1. Property Disputes

Conflicts relating to ownership, possession, title, transfer, inheritance and rights over immovable property.

Applicable Acts:
  • Transfer of Property Act, 1882
  • Registration Act, 1908
  • Indian Stamp Act, 1899
  • Specific Relief Act, 1963
  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908
2. Partition Suits

Division of jointly owned or ancestral property among co-owners.

Applicable Acts:
  • Hindu Succession Act, 1956
  • Indian Succession Act, 1925
  • Partition Act, 1893
  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908
3. Recovery Suits

Legal action to recover unpaid money, debts, loans or property.

Applicable Acts:
  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881
  • Limitation Act, 1963
4. Specific Performance

Courts compel parties to fulfill contractual obligations where compensation is insufficient.

Applicable Acts:
  • Specific Relief Act, 1963
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Transfer of Property Act, 1882
5. Injunction Matters

Court orders restraining or compelling actions to protect legal rights.

Applicable Acts:
  • Specific Relief Act, 1963
  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Order XXXIX)
  • Transfer of Property Act, 1882
6. Breach of Contract

Failure to perform contractual obligations. Remedies include compensation or specific performance.

Applicable Acts:
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Specific Relief Act, 1963
  • Sale of Goods Act, 1930
  • Limitation Act, 1963
7. Consumer Disputes

Cases involving defective goods, deficient services, or unfair trade practices.

Applicable Acts:
  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019
  • Legal Metrology Act, 2009
  • Sale of Goods Act, 1930
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
