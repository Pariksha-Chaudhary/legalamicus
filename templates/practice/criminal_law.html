Criminal Law Practice Areas

1. Bail (Regular / Anticipatory)

Legal assistance in securing regular bail after arrest and anticipatory bail before arrest in non-bailable offences.

Applicable Laws:
  • Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Sections 437, 438, 439)
  • Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023 (New Criminal Procedure Law)
2. FIR Quashing

Petitions filed before the High Court to quash false or malicious FIRs under inherent jurisdiction.

Applicable Laws:
  • Section 482, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
  • Article 226, Constitution of India
3. Cheque Bounce (NI Act 138)

Legal proceedings for dishonour of cheque due to insufficient funds or account closure.

Applicable Laws:
  • Section 138, Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881
  • Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
4. Cyber Crime

Cases involving online fraud, hacking, identity theft, data breaches, and digital offences.

Applicable Laws:
  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • Indian Penal Code, 1860 / Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023
5. Fraud & Cheating

Defense and prosecution in cases involving financial fraud, cheating, criminal breach of trust and deception.

Applicable Laws:
  • Sections 415, 420 IPC / Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita
  • Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988
6. Domestic Violence Defense

Legal defense in cases filed under domestic violence allegations and related criminal complaints.

Applicable Laws:
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • Section 498A IPC
7. Trial Representation

Comprehensive criminal trial defense including evidence handling, cross-examination, arguments, and appeals.

Applicable Laws:
  • Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
  • Indian Evidence Act, 1872
  • Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023
