Family Law Practice Areas

1. Divorce (Mutual & Contested)

Legal assistance in mutual consent divorce and contested divorce proceedings based on cruelty, desertion, adultery, or other grounds.

Applicable Laws:
  • Hindu Marriage Act, 1955
  • Special Marriage Act, 1954
  • Indian Divorce Act, 1869
2. Child Custody

Representation in custody disputes, visitation rights, and guardianship matters prioritizing child welfare.

Applicable Laws:
  • Guardians and Wards Act, 1890
  • Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956
3. Maintenance

Claim or defense in maintenance proceedings for spouse, child, or parents under family and criminal law provisions.

Applicable Laws:
  • Section 125, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
  • Hindu Adoption and Maintenance Act, 1956
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
4. Domestic Violence

Legal protection against physical, emotional, verbal, and economic abuse within domestic relationships.

Applicable Laws:
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • Section 498A IPC
5. Dowry Cases

Legal action in dowry harassment, cruelty, and related criminal complaints or defense matters.

Applicable Laws:
  • Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961
  • Section 304B IPC (Dowry Death)
  • Section 498A IPC
6. Restitution of Conjugal Rights

Petitions filed when one spouse withdraws from marital cohabitation without reasonable cause.

Applicable Laws:
  • Section 9, Hindu Marriage Act, 1955
  • Special Marriage Act, 1954
